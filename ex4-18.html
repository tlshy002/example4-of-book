Rise of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles
(also known as Rise of the TMNT and ROTTMNT)
is an American animated television series based on
the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles characters created
by Kevin Eastman and Peter Laird. Developed and executive
produced by Andy Suriano and Ant Ward, the series premiered
on Nickelodeon on July 20, 2018. Premieres later moved to Nicktoons,
which aired new episodes until August 7, 2020.
